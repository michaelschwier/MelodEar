HOW TO PLAY×

Guess the secret notes of the melody that you hear. Enter your guess on the keyboard, starting with the given first note.

After each guess, the tile colors will change to show which notes were correct and which were wrong. Before starting your next guess you are allowed to replay the secret melody and your latest guess one time.

Reach the next level by guessing the correct melody in five or less tries. Can you finish all five levels?
