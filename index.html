HOW TO PLAY×

Guess the secret melody that you hear. For reference the first note of the melody is already revealed.

Enter your melody guess on the keyboard, including the first note.

After each guess, the color of tiles will change to show which notes were correct and which were wrong. Before starting your next guess you are allowed to replay the secret melody and your last guess one time.

Reach the next level by guessing the correct melody in five or less tries. There are five levels, the first has a two-note melody, the last a six-note melody.
