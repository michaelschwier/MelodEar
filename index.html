HOW TO PLAY×

Guess the secret notes of the melody that you hear. Enter your guess on the keyboard, starting with the already revealed first note.

After each guess, the color of tiles will change to show which notes were correct and which were wrong. Before starting your next guess you are allowed to replay the secret melody and your latest guess one time.

Reach the next level by guessing the correct melody in five or less tries. Can you finish all five levels?

COOKIE POLICY

We are only using cookies that are necessary for the game to function as intended. We absolutely value your privacy and hence we don't use tracking cookies and we don't collect personal data.

To fully enjoy playing MelodEar please accept the essential cookies.

If you reject even the essential cookies, some features of the game will not work.

BETA TEST

This is the beta test version of MelodEar. The game is currently still under development but you are welcome to try it out already. However, be aware that you might encounter some weird behaviour or bugs here and there. Also some features are different from how they'll be in the final version. A particular difference is that for testing purposes you get to play a new game every 5 minutes. The final version will have only one game per day.

If you enounter any bugs or issues, please write us at betatest@melodear.net.

Finally we'd like to ask you to please not share this site or your results with others just yet. Please wait until the official release of MelodEar.
