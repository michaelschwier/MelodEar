HOW TO PLAY

Guess the secret melody that you hear. For reference the first note of the melody is already revealed.

Enter your melody guess on the keyboard, including the first note.

After each guess, the color of tiles will change to show which notes were correct and which were wrong.

Reach the next level by guessing the correct melody in 5 or less tries. There are 5 levels, the first has a 2-note melody, the last a 6-note melody.
